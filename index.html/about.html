About Us

Mahaveer Seba Sangha, a temple trust with a profound commitment to community welfare, was established five years ago with the vision of serving and uplifting the people of our society. Officially registered with the government on October 5, 2018, the trust has steadily evolved into a beacon of hope and support for those in need. Guided by its core principles, Mahaveer Seva Sangha aims to alleviate poverty and contribute to the overall well-being of the community it serves. Since its inception, Mahaveer Seva Sangha has embarked on various initiatives to fulfill its mission. The trust has actively engaged in philanthropic activities, providing assistance to underprivileged individuals and families facing economic challenges. Through the years, Mahaveer Seva Sangha has become a source of solace for those in distress, offering not only material support but also spiritual guidance. As a testament to its commitment to the community, Mahaveer Seva Sangha has undertaken the admirable task of constructing a Sriram temple adjacent to the existing Hanuman temple. This visionary project aims to create a spiritual sanctuary that not only fosters religious devotion but also serves as a center for cultural and social activities. The decision to build the Sriram temple reflects the trust's dedication to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage embedded in the community. The proposed Sriram temple holds great significance, especially in its proximity to the Hanuman temple. Both temples together will create a harmonious spiritual environment, fostering a sense of unity and reverence among devotees. The trustees believe that this addition will further strengthen the spiritual bond within the community, offering devotees a comprehensive space for worship and reflection. To bring this vision to fruition, Mahaveer Seva Sangha humbly seeks the support of the community. The trust acknowledges that such transformative projects require the collective efforts of individuals who share the same values and aspirations. As part of its fundraising efforts, Mahaveer Seva Sangha invites people to contribute to the temple trust through donations. These funds will be channeled towards the construction, maintenance, and various community-oriented activities associated with the Sriram temple. The call for donations is not just a financial appeal; it is an invitation to actively participate in the creation of a sacred space that will benefit the entire community. Every contribution, no matter the size, plays a vital role in the realization of this noble endeavor. Mahaveer Seva Sangha believes that by uniting in purpose, the community can achieve remarkable feats, transcending the limitations that poverty may impose. The trust envisions the Sriram temple not only as a place of worship but also as a catalyst for positive change. Through its activities and outreach programs, Mahaveer Seva Sangha aims to uplift the socio-economic conditions of the less privileged, creating a ripple effect that extends far beyond the temple premises. In conclusion, Mahaveer Seva Sangha stands as a testament to the transformative power of collective action and community service. Established just five years ago, the trust has already made significant strides in fulfilling its mission. The journey towards building the Sriram temple is an embodiment of the trust's unwavering commitment to the well-being of society. As it invites contributions from the community, Mahaveer Seva Sangha looks forward to the day when the Sriram temple becomes a symbol of hope, unity, and spiritual enlightenment for generations to come.
